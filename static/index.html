About

The Technology

Our AI detection tool uses advanced deep learning algorithms to analyze pixel patterns and visual artifacts that are typically invisible to the human eye. By training on thousands of AI-Generated and Human-Created images, our model can identify subtle inconsistencies that reveal an image's true origin.

Why It Matters

As AI-generated images become increasingly realistic and widespread, the ability to distinguish between authentic and synthetic content is crucial. Our tool helps combat misinformation, protect digital authenticity, and maintain trust in visual media during a time when the line between real and artificial is rapidly blurring.
